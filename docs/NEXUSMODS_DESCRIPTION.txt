[center][size=5][b]Fortune Teller[/b][/size]

[i]Hover over cards to see what they'll do before you use them.[/i][/center]

Fortune Teller shows you the outcome of random-result cards right in the hover popup — no guessing needed.

[list]
[*]See what a card will generate or target before using it
[*]Card effect description moves to a small side popup
[*]Collection view stays unchanged
[*]Hovering never affects gameplay — your random outcomes stay the same
[*]Per-card toggles and display options in mod settings
[/list]

[size=4][b]Supported Cards[/b][/size]

[b]Tarot[/b]
[list]
[*]Wheel of Fortune (shows target joker, or "Nope!" if it would fail)
[*]The Emperor, The High Priestess (shows all cards that would be created)
[*]Judgement, The Soul, Wraith
[/list]

[b]Spectral[/b]
[list]
[*]Aura, Sigil, Ouija, Hex, Ectoplasm, Ankh
[*]Familiar, Grim, Incantation, Immolate
[/list]

[b]Joker[/b]
[list]
[*]8 Ball, Misprint, Madness, Riff-Raff, Hallucination
[*]Vagabond, Superposition, Cartomancer, Sixth Sense, Seance, Certificate, Perkeo
[*]Invisible Joker
[*]Blueprint and Brainstorm show the copied joker's prediction when hovered
[/list]

[b]Seal[/b]
[list]
[*]Purple Seal (highlight cards in hand to preview the next Tarot outcomes)
[/list]
